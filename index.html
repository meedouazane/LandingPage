About

Healthy Body came to life because I wanted something special for my health journey. It's not just about meeting school requirements but about creating a tool that resonates with people on a personal level. I realized there wasn't a user-friendly platform that not only calculates BMI but also guides you towards better health decisions.

This project reflects my own ups and downs in health. From the initial idea to building the platform, every step was about making a tool that feels like a companion on your wellness journey.
The main aim was clear: create a simple, motivating, and educational experience beyond a regular BMI calculator.
